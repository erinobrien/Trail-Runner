About the App


Trail Runner is the running app you didn't know you needed. Explore Wellington city through a new set of eyes as you discover trails you never knew were right in your backyard. Trail Runner will guide you from your current location, directly to the trail head and along the trail itself. With visual and voice directions, no trail is too hard to find!


Runners of all skill levels and experience can use Trail Runner. The interface is designed with simplicity in mind, making it easier than ever to pick a route and run!


Trail Runner wants to get you on the trails quicker and easier than ever before, so you have more time to focus on your run and explore Wellington city in a completely new way!



Key Features

Audio Directions

Turn audio directions on to help you get to the trail hands-free

Filters

Filter your search to find a trail best to suited to you

Location

Shows the exact distance from your current location to the trail head

Explore

Explore the nature Wellington City has to offer

Health

Exercising out in nature will keep your mind and body happy!

Simplicity

Easy to read icons and filters make app navigation enjoyable for everyone

Downloadable Maps

Download any trail maps to be viewed without wifi or data


Review

Read other runners trail reviews as well as writing your own

Inspiration

My initial inspiration for designing Trail Runner came from the real problems I had experienced when attempting to run trails I had never been on before. Already being a step ahead in terms of user experience and research, I thought this was a good opportunity to design an app that I personally would love to use. Not everyone likes to run or exercise outdoors but I used this as an extra challenge component - to entice even the most resistent.

I conducted research by downloading and using different apps and looking on a range of websites. I started to create a list of different components that worked well within each app or website and tried to incorporate it into my design. With the end goal of creating a seamless and simple, yet detailed app that even the least tech-savvy person could navigate. This project was a great way to get back out on the trails and gain inspiration from real user experience, which made the design process very enjoyable.
